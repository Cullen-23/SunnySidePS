Welcome to Sunnyside Primary School

Located in Tega Zone, Nabbingo, Kampala — we are dedicated to holistic education that nurtures mind, body, and character.

At Sunnyside, we believe every child has a unique potential waiting to be discovered. Our experienced teachers, child-centered approach, and vibrant learning environment ensure that each student grows academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

We combine the Ugandan curriculum with modern teaching methodologies to prepare learners for a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

Our 7 Aims & Objectives

We are committed to providing a dignified, enriching education for every child.

1

Safe & Happy Environment

Where children feel secure, fostering a thirst for learning through exploration and intellectual debate.

2

Dignified Education

Education free from humiliation, intimidation and unhealthy competition for all learners.

3

Individualized Attention

Empowering every child to reach their full potential regardless of academic strength.

4

Reading Culture

Cultivating a love for reading as a stepping stone to lifelong learning.

5

Culture of Excellence

Fostering excellence in young minds through high expectations and support.

6

Compassionate Individuals

Nurturing compassion and genuine care for the global community.

7

Values & Character

Through social emotional learning, we cultivate emotional intelligence and strong character.
