Welcome to Sunnyside Primary School

Located in Tega Zone, Nabbingo, Kampala — we are dedicated to holistic education that nurtures mind, body, and character.

At Sunnyside, we believe every child has a unique potential waiting to be discovered. Our experienced teachers, child-centered approach, and vibrant learning environment ensure that each student grows academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

We combine the Ugandan curriculum with modern teaching methodologies to prepare learners for a rapidly changing world. Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities designed to inspire creativity and foster a love for learning.

With a focus on individualized attention, we ensure that every child receives the support they need to reach their full potential, regardless of their academic starting point.
